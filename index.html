About AlgoVerse

AlgoVerse is an innovative educational platform designed to make algorithm learning interactive and engaging. We specialize in visualizing Dijkstra's shortest path algorithm, one of the most fundamental algorithms in computer science. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical understanding through immersive, visual experiences that make complex concepts accessible to learners at all levels.
